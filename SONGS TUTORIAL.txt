- Make a folder called "Songs"
- Subfolders for each song "Song1", "Song2", etc
- Take 32 bar chunks out of the song for use with the application.
- Each song can have a major and minor versions of all stems except for drums, in the ".ogg" format. (vocals_major.ogg, vocals_minor.ogg, bass_major.ogg, bass_minor.ogg, drums.ogg, etc)
- Follow the meta.json example template and put that in the folder as well.
- You should have 8 files in total.
